LOMAS ALTAMIRA 33

El complejo es una excelente inversión con potencial para desarrollo para uso institucional, habitacional, hotelero o de oficinas corporativas.

Con un área total de terreno de 7,684 m2 y construcción de 9,239 m2, el complejo consta con 4 edificios (A, B, C y D) con espacios de recepción, salas de espera, reuniones y usos múltiples; espacios amplios para oficinas abiertas, 10 oficinas independientes y 12 oficinas con SS privado, área de archivo, sistema de cómputo, además de amplios espacios para adaptación futura y adecuarlo a sus requerimientos, todos rodeados de hermosos jardines con sistema de riego automático.

Además, el complejo cuenta con un amplio parqueo interno con capacidad de 50 a 75 vehículos y áreas para almacenaje con zona de descarga.

La propiedad cuenta con amenidades como: elevador, purificador de agua con sistema de filtración ultravioleta, calentador de agua con paneles solares, tanques térmicos de agua, tanque y dispensador de combustible, planta eléctrica de emergencia, cuartos fríos, planta telefónica, cctv, caseta de vigilancia, portón eléctrico; todos con un excelente mantenimiento y condiciones.

VECINDARIO LINDO

The Towers has embodied the architectural elegance and historical significance of Brooklyn Heights for nearly a century. Ideally situated within this picturesque landmarked neighborhood, it spans a city block along the east side of Willow Street, between Clark and Pineapple streets. The Towers is one block away from the Brooklyn Heights Promenade, a destination highlight for locals and tourists. Nearby Brooklyn Bridge Park offers premier outdoor community spaces, parkland, and modern sports fields along the waterfront. The Towers is also a short jaunt from Henry and Montague streets, the neighborhood’s main commercial districts for shopping, dining, and personal and professional services. The adjacent communities of DUMBO and Downtown Brooklyn have become centers for commerce, technology, education, and culture, offering Brooklyn Heights residents additional world-class amenities close by.

TRANSPORTE CONVENIENTE

The Towers is attractively located a few steps from the Clark Street (2/3) subway station, providing a quick commute to Lower Manhattan’s Financial District. A brief stroll from the property are the High Street (A/C), Court Street (R, 4/5), and York Street (F) stations, all just one train stop to Lower Manhattan and Downtown Brooklyn. In close proximity to The Towers are major transportation thoroughfares, such as the Brooklyn-Queens Expressway, Brooklyn Bridge, and Manhattan Bridge. Other commuter favorites include ferry/water taxi service, Citi Bike, bus lines, and bike paths.
